About Me

At the intersection of
business, technology and People.

I grew up in Nigeria and now live in Sweden, where I'm building my career in product. I genuinely enjoy making people feel good, whether through small moments, positive energy, or simply showing up for the people around me. I'm confident leading conversations, comfortable taking initiative, and naturally drawn to creating clarity in environments where ideas, priorities, or teams may feel scattered.

My approach to working with teams is built on transparency, openness, and trust. I believe people do their best work when expectations are clear, communication is honest, and everyone understands the direction. I enjoy facilitating alignment, bringing people together around a shared goal, and creating the kind of structure that helps teams move forward with confidence.

I would not describe myself as totally organised. My thinking can sometimes start out messy because I do have many ideas and moving pieces in my head at once. Over time, I've learned to manage this by writing things down, structuring my notes, breaking work into chunks, and consistently revisiting my plans so nothing slips. That has helped me stay thorough, focused, and accountable.

I'm highly collaborative and naturally empathetic, qualities that have helped me build strong relationships with diverse stakeholders and teammates. Whether I'm stepping into a Scrum Master capacity to keep delivery on track, diving into user research to understand real needs, or contributing to product strategy, I bring a disciplined, goal-oriented mindset to every team I manage.

Outside of work, I enjoy staying active and living a healthy lifestyle. Fitness, outdoor walks, and movement help me reset and stay grounded.
